# Recipe Graph

The **Graph** tab in the Crafting Admin panel is a planned feature, not currently available.
It will display an interactive visual map of how your recipes are connected through shared components.
The goal is to help you to understand dependencies, spot circular crafting chains, and plan your crafting system.
